About Us

"THE ARM OF BATSTATEU IN MAPPING"

GADC aims to create interdisciplinary collaborations for GIS-technology solutions for research, education, and community. The center offers software and hardware for GIS-technology driven research and conducts training to researchers


It targets to produce GIS-technology solutions for research, education, and community by building and maintaining GIS databases. The Center will utilize GIS software to analyze spatial and non-spatial information as well as creating maps for projects and study. Helping individuals inside or outside the University while growing in the field of Geographics Information System. GIS Applications Development Center aims to provide individuals inside and outside the University knowledge and principles about this computer system. GADC offers Data Acquisition using Drone Imagery (Aerial) and/or Photography. The Center also provides Consultation for individual or group project and/or study assessment inside or outside the University. Services like Data Processing, Map creation (based on data acquired or provided), map printing, providing available maps (created by the GADC) and image interpretation.
